AnkiPA is an addon that helps you practice your pronunciation. Your voice is recorded and compared to the reference text to evaluate the pronunciation. Within seconds you receive an overview containing accuracy, fluency and pronunciation score and which words you pronounced correctly and what mistakes you commited.



It processes voice data locally on your machine to deliver pronunciation assessments. No data is sent to any server, ensuring your privacy and security. The addon uses the Vosk speech recognition library to transcribe your spoken words, the g2p_en library to convert the reference text into phonemes, and the rapidfuzz library to compare the phonetic representations and calculate pronunciation scores.
